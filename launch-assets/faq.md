# Scriptorfi FAQ

## Pricing

### How much does transcription cost?
- Human transcription starts at $0.50 per minute.
- Auto transcription starts at $0.07 per minute.

### Are there add-ons?
- Yes. For human transcription, optional add-ons include:
- Verbatim: +$0.20 per minute
- Rush order: +$0.50 per minute

### Do you support multiple currencies?
- Yes. Checkout supports multiple currencies and shows your estimated total before payment.

## Turnaround

### How fast will I get my transcript?
- Auto transcription is typically delivered in minutes.
- Human transcription is typically delivered in 24 to 72 hours, depending on file length, quality, and selected options.

### What affects turnaround time?
- Audio quality
- File duration
- Number of speakers
- Background noise
- Rush order selection

## Revisions

### Do I get revisions?
- Yes. If your transcript does not match your submitted instructions, you can request a revision.

### How do I request a revision?
- Reply to your order confirmation or contact support with:
- Your order reference
- The sections to revise
- The exact correction request

### How quickly are revisions handled?
- Most revision requests are reviewed within 24 hours.

## Refunds

### What is your refund policy?
- If work has not started and no transcript has been delivered, you can request a full refund.
- If a transcript has been delivered, we first offer a revision to resolve quality issues.
- If we cannot reasonably resolve a confirmed quality issue, a partial or full refund may be approved.

### Are payment processing fees refundable?
- Third-party processing fees may be non-refundable depending on the payment provider.

### How do I request a refund?
- Contact support with your order reference and reason for the request.

## Support

### How can I contact support?
- Email: support@scriptorfi.com

### What should I include in my support message?
- Order reference
- File name
- Clear description of your request
- Deadline (if urgent)
